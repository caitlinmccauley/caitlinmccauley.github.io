Call Your Reps!

The simple way to excercise your right to free speech!

Why Call?

    1) Calling members of Congress is the most effective way to invoke change
    2) Staffers pass the message (and call tally) along to representatives
    3) Unhappy consituents can swap reps to change their stance
    4) You have the opportunity to educate and tell your story and reasoning
    5) Someone has to talk to you, and large call volumes bring rep offices to a halt!

What's Below?

    Ever wanted to call your representative and voice your concerns about a critical issue that affects you...
    But had absolutely no idea what to say? Look no further! Call your reps gives you easy to read up to date scripts you can use for your phonecalls So you can excercise your first Ammendment right, voice your opinions, and avoid the awkwardness of not knowing what to say!

How does this work?

1) Give us your name (we'll throw it in the script for you below)


2) Give us your zipcode (it's important they know you're a constituent)
